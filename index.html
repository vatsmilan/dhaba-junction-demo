Our Story

Established in 1995, Dhaba Junction brings the authentic taste of roadside dhabas to the bustling streets of Karol Bagh, New Delhi. What started as a small eatery has grown into a beloved culinary destination for locals and food lovers.

We pride ourselves on using traditional cooking methods, fresh ingredients, and recipes passed down through generations. Our clay oven (tandoor) and secret spice blends create flavors that transport you straight to the heart of Punjab.
